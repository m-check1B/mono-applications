## üéØ Response Principles

### Do's
- ‚úÖ Acknowledge the concern (validate emotion)
- ‚úÖ Provide specific proof (not generic claims)
- ‚úÖ Show, don't tell (demo > description)
- ‚úÖ Honest about limitations (builds trust)
- ‚úÖ Offer workarounds (helpful even if not perfect)
- ‚úÖ Quantify value (numbers > adjectives)

### Don'ts
- ‚ùå Dismiss or minimize concern
- ‚ùå Over-promise on vague timeline
- ‚ùå Bad-mouth competitors
- ‚ùå Get defensive
- ‚ùå Feature dump as response
- ‚ùå Use jargon or tech-speak

### Response Template

```
1. Validate
   "I totally understand - [specific concern] is important."

2. Explain why
   "Here's why that happens: [root cause]"

3. What we're doing
   "Here's our approach: [specific solution]"

4. Proof
   "Let me show you: [demo/data/testimonial]"

5. Workaround (if needed)
   "In the meantime: [temporary solution]"

6. Next step
   "Try [specific action] and see if it works for you."
```
