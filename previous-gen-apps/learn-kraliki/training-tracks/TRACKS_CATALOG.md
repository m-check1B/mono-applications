## 1. Healthcare: AI for Healthcare Professionals

**HIPAA-Compliant AI Implementation for Clinical Excellence**

### Target Audience
- Healthcare IT professionals
- Clinical informaticists
- Medical directors and administrators
- Data scientists in healthcare
- Compliance officers
- Nursing leadership
- Radiology and pathology teams

### Learning Objectives
1. Understand AI capabilities and limitations in clinical settings while maintaining patient safety
2. Implement AI solutions that comply with HIPAA, GDPR, and healthcare-specific regulations
3. Evaluate and deploy clinical decision support systems with appropriate human oversight
4. Manage patient data privacy and security in AI-powered healthcare applications
5. Develop governance frameworks for responsible AI use in medical contexts

### Module Outline (40 hours total)

| Module | Title | Duration | Key Topics |
|--------|-------|----------|------------|
| 1 | AI Foundations for Healthcare | 6 hrs | AI basics, current applications, case studies |
| 2 | HIPAA and Healthcare AI Compliance | 8 hrs | Privacy Rule, Security Rule, BAAs, de-identification, FDA guidance |
| 3 | Clinical Decision Support Systems | 8 hrs | CDS types, EHR integration, alert fatigue, override protocols |
| 4 | Patient Data and AI Ethics | 6 hrs | Consent, bias, health equity, transparency |
| 5 | AI-Powered Diagnostics | 6 hrs | Medical imaging, validation, quality assurance |
| 6 | Implementation and Governance | 6 hrs | Governance committee, vendor selection, pilot design, monitoring |

### Duration & Format
- **Total Duration:** 40 hours
- **Recommended Schedule:** 8 weeks, 5 hours/week
- **Delivery Options:** Self-paced, Instructor-led, Blended
- **Live Components:** Weekly office hours, expert panels, peer discussions

### Certification: Certified Healthcare AI Professional (CHAIP)
- Complete all modules (80% assessment score minimum)
- Submit capstone: AI Implementation Plan
- Pass final exam (100 questions, 70% minimum)
- Complete hands-on lab exercises
- Valid for 2 years

### Pricing
| Option | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Individual | EUR 2,500 |
| Corporate (10+ seats) | EUR 1,800/seat |
