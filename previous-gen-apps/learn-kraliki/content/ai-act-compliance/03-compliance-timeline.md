# Compliance Timeline & Deadlines

## Implementation Timeline

The AI Act was officially adopted and became **enforceable on February 2, 2025**.

### Key Milestones

| Date | Milestone | Impact |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Feb 2, 2025** | AI Act enters into force | Prohibited AI systems banned immediately |
| **Feb 2, 2025** | General purpose AI rules apply | Transparency obligations for foundation models |
| **Feb 2, 2026** | High-risk AI rules fully apply | Full compliance requirements for high-risk systems |
| **Feb 2, 2027** | Governance rules apply | AI offices, notified bodies fully operational |
