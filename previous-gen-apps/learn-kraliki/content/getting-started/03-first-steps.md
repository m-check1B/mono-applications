# Your First Steps

Now that you understand the dashboard, let's take some practical steps to set up your Kraliki workspace.

## Step 1: Complete Your Profile

A complete profile helps your team recognize you and enables personalized AI recommendations.

1. Click your **avatar** in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile Settings**
3. Fill in:
   - Full name
   - Role/Title
   - Department
   - Time zone
   - Notification preferences

## Step 2: Explore Focus

Focus is your productivity companion. Let's create your first task:

1. Click **Focus** in the navigation
2. Click the **+ New Task** button
3. Enter a task title: "Complete Kraliki onboarding"
4. Set priority to **High**
5. Click **Create**

Congratulations! You've created your first task.

## Step 3: Set Up Speak (For Managers)

If you manage a team, set up Speak for voice feedback:

1. Navigate to **Speak**
2. Click **Create Survey**
3. Name it "Team Check-in"
4. Add 2-3 questions for your team
5. Send invitations

> **Note:** Speak conversations are anonymous by default to encourage honest feedback.

## Step 4: Enable AI Agent

Kraliki's AI agents can help automate routine tasks:

1. Go to **Settings** > **AI Agents**
2. Enable the **Assistant Agent**
3. Review the permissions
4. Click **Activate**

The agent will now help with:
- Task prioritization
- Meeting summaries
- Email drafts
- Data analysis

## Step 5: Invite Your Team

Collaboration makes Kraliki more powerful:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Team**
2. Click **Invite Members**
3. Enter email addresses
4. Select their roles
5. Send invitations
