# Welcome to Kraliki

Welcome to **Kraliki** - your AI-powered business operating system. This course will help you get started with the platform and understand how to make the most of its capabilities.

## What is Kraliki?

Kraliki is an ecosystem of AI-powered tools designed to help businesses:

- **Automate operations** with intelligent agents
- **Improve employee experience** with voice-first feedback
- **Boost productivity** with focus tools
- **Train teams** with structured learning paths

## The Kraliki Ecosystem

Kraliki consists of several integrated products:

| Product | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Focus** | Productivity and task management |
| **Speak** | Employee voice feedback platform |
| **Learn** | Training and onboarding (you are here!) |
| **Voice** | AI-powered call center |
| **Lab** | Deployment automation |

## What You Will Learn

In this course, you will:

1. Take a tour of the Kraliki dashboard
2. Set up your account and preferences
3. Learn how to navigate between products
4. Understand how AI agents work
5. Complete your first task
