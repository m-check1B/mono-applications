## Risk Mitigation

### Common Challenges

**Challenge 1: Lack of Resources**
**Solution:** Prioritize systems, stagger implementations

**Challenge 2: Technical Complexity**
**Solution:** Engage external experts (Verduona)

**Challenge 3: Stakeholder Resistance**
**Solution:** Executive sponsorship, clear communication

**Challenge 4: Unclear Requirements**
**Solution:** Early engagement with notified bodies

### Contingency Planning

**If you cannot meet deadlines:**
1. Document all efforts
2. Demonstrate good faith compliance
3. Request extensions from regulators
4. Consider temporarily suspending systems

**If you discover prohibited AI:**
1. Stop immediately
2. Document shutdown
3. Find compliant alternative
4. Report to regulators (if required)
