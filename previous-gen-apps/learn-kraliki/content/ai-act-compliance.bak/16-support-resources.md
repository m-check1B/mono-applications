## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How long does compliance take?**
A: 3-12 months depending on system complexity and starting point.

**Q: What if I miss the deadline?**
A: Document your efforts, demonstrate good faith, request extensions.

**Q: Do I need external help?**
A: Recommended for high-risk systems. Verduona provides affordable support.

**Q: Can I do this myself?**
A: Possible for minimal/limited-risk. High-risk requires expert support.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What documentation do I need?**
A: 100+ pages for high-risk systems. Templates provided.

**Q: How do I know if my AI is high-risk?**
A: Use the decision tree from workshop exercise.

**Q: What is a conformity assessment?**
A: Third-party certification process for high-risk AI systems.

### Business Questions

**Q: How much will compliance cost?**
A: €33,000-67,000 per high-risk system typically.

**Q: Is there a quick fix?**
A: No, but Verduona streamlines the process significantly.

**Q: What's my risk of non-compliance?**
A: Up to €30M in fines plus business disruption.
