## Your 3 Quick Wins (Start Here)

### 1. Experiment with Free AI Tools
Start building AI literacy without any investment:
- Use ChatGPT/Claude for email drafting and document summarization
- Try Canva's AI features for marketing materials
- Use Otter.ai for meeting transcription

**Time: 30 minutes to set up | Cost: Free**

### 2. Audit Your Repetitive Tasks
Create a simple list of tasks that:
- Happen repeatedly (daily/weekly)
- Follow clear rules
- Take significant time
- Are boring but necessary

This becomes your AI opportunity list.

**Time: 1 hour | Cost: Free**

### 3. Centralize One Data Source
Pick your most important data (customer list, inventory, sales) and:
- Move it from spreadsheets to a proper tool
- Ensure one source of truth
- Make it accessible to those who need it

**Time: 2-4 hours | Cost: Free-$50/month**
