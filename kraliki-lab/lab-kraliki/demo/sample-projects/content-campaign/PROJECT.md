# GrowthHQ - Content Campaign Project

## Project Overview

**Client:** GrowthHQ Marketing Agency (Demo)
**Project:** Q1 2026 Content Campaign
**Scope:** 4 blog posts + social content + email sequence
**Theme:** AI Productivity for Marketing Teams

## Campaign Brief

GrowthHQ needs a complete content campaign for their client's product launch. The campaign should drive awareness and generate leads through SEO-optimized content, social media distribution, and email nurturing.

## Content Calendar

| Week | Content Type | Topic | Channel |
|------|--------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Blog post | "5 AI Tools Every Marketer Needs in 2026" | Blog, LinkedIn |
| 2 | Blog post | "How to 10x Content Output Without Hiring" | Blog, Twitter |
| 3 | Blog post | "The Marketer's Guide to AI Prompt Engineering" | Blog, Newsletter |
| 4 | Blog post | "Case Study: How [Client] Scaled Content 400%" | Blog, LinkedIn, Sales |

## Blog Post Template

Each post should include:
- **Headline** (60 chars max, power words)
- **Meta description** (155 chars max)
- **Introduction** (100-150 words, hook + thesis)
- **Body** (1,200-1,500 words, H2 sections)
- **Conclusion** (100 words, CTA)
- **Author bio** (50 words)

## Social Media Requirements

### LinkedIn (per post)
- 1 long-form text post (1,300 chars)
- 3 engagement hooks as comments
- 1 carousel concept (5-7 slides)

### Twitter/X (per post)
- 1 thread (5-7 tweets)
- 1 single tweet summary
- Quote tweet angle

## Email Sequence

### Email 1: Welcome
- Subject: "Welcome to the AI productivity movement"
- Purpose: Set expectations, deliver lead magnet

### Email 2: Education
- Subject: "The 16x productivity framework"
- Purpose: Teach core concept

### Email 3: Social Proof
- Subject: "How [Company] did it in 30 days"
- Purpose: Case study, build credibility

### Email 4: CTA
- Subject: "Your turn?"
- Purpose: Drive demo bookings

## Brand Voice

- **Tone:** Confident but approachable
- **Avoid:** Corporate jargon, buzzwords
- **Include:** Real examples, specific numbers
- **Perspective:** First person plural ("we")
