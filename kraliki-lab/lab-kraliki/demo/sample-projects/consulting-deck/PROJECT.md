# NorthStar Consulting - Market Analysis Project

## Project Overview

**Client:** NorthStar Consulting (Demo)
**Project:** AI Adoption Market Analysis
**Deliverable:** Executive presentation deck
**Audience:** C-level executives, board members

## Project Brief

NorthStar Consulting needs a comprehensive market analysis on AI adoption in mid-market B2B companies. The output should be a polished presentation deck suitable for board-level discussions.

## Research Questions

1. What is the current state of AI adoption in mid-market B2B?
2. What are the primary barriers to adoption?
3. Which use cases are showing the highest ROI?
4. What does the competitive landscape look like?
5. What are the key recommendations for NorthStar's clients?

## Deck Structure

### Slide 1: Title
- AI Adoption in Mid-Market B2B: 2025 Landscape
- NorthStar Consulting logo
- Date and confidentiality notice

### Slide 2: Executive Summary
- 3 key findings
- 1 critical recommendation
- Expected timeline for market evolution

### Slide 3: Market Context
- Size of mid-market B2B segment
- Current tech stack typical in this segment
- Digital transformation progress

### Slide 4: Adoption Rates
- Chart: AI adoption by company size
- Chart: AI adoption by industry
- Year-over-year growth

### Slide 5: Barriers to Adoption
- Technical barriers (ranked)
- Organizational barriers (ranked)
- Cost/ROI concerns

### Slide 6: High-ROI Use Cases
- Top 5 use cases with ROI ranges
- Implementation complexity matrix
- Time to value

### Slide 7: Competitive Analysis
- Major AI tool vendors
- Positioning map
- Emerging players to watch

### Slide 8: Recommendations
- 3 strategic recommendations
- Quick wins vs long-term plays
- Resource requirements

### Slide 9: Next Steps
- Proposed engagement model
- Timeline
- Investment ranges

### Slide 10: Appendix
- Methodology
- Sources
- Contact information

## Quality Standards

- All statistics must have sources
- Charts should be described (for AI generation)
- Professional tone, no jargon
- Each slide has ONE key message
- Max 6 bullet points per slide
