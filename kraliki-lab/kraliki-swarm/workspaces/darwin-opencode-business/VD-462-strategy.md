## Product Strategy

### Phase 1: Academy (Q2 2026 - Q3 2026)

**Core Courses (3):**

#### 1. **AI Fundamentals for Business** (VD-463 dependency)
- Target: Professionals, managers, HR, finance
- Duration: 4 weeks (online self-paced)
- Content: AI literacy, use cases, ROI calculation, safety
- Price: €99-149 (individual), €499-799 (corporate team)
- Language: Czech (primary), English (supplement)

#### 2. **Prompt Engineering Mastery**
- Target: Knowledge workers, marketers, support teams
- Duration: 3 weeks
- Content: RTC framework, advanced prompting, tool selection
- Price: €149-249 (individual), €799-1,299 (corporate)
- Language: Czech (primary), English (supplement)

#### 3. **Industry Implementation Tracks** (VD-464)
- Target: Healthcare, finance, manufacturing
- Duration: 6 weeks (project-based)
- Content: Industry-specific use cases, compliance, implementation
- Price: €249-399 (individual), €1,499-2,499 (corporate)
- Language: Czech (primary), English (supplement)

**Total Academy Revenue Potential:** 10 students × €300 avg = €3K MRR

### Phase 2: Advisory (Q4 2026)
- Target: Academy graduates + SMEs
- Pricing: €1,500-3,000 per engagement
- Offer: AI workflow audit, implementation roadmap

### Phase 3: Labs (2027+)
- Target: Advisory clients
- Pricing: €5K+ setup + MRR
- Offer: Lab by Kraliki deployment
