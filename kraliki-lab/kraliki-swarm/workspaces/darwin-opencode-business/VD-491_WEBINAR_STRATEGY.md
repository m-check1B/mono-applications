## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **‚ùå Not promoting enough**
   - **Fix:** Minimum 3 LinkedIn posts + 2 emails + 50 cold outreach emails

2. **‚ùå Making it too long**
   - **Fix:** Keep to 45-60 minutes max

3. **‚ùå Too much theory, not enough practicality**
   - **Fix:** Live demos, case studies, specific tools

4. **‚ùå No clear CTA**
   - **Fix:** Have a specific, time-limited offer

5. **‚ùå Not following up**
   - **Fix:** At least 3 emails in 48 hours

6. **‚ùå Poor audio/video quality**
   - **Fix:** Test equipment beforehand, use good mic

7. **‚ùå Ignoring questions**
   - **Fix:** Answer questions live and follow up via email
