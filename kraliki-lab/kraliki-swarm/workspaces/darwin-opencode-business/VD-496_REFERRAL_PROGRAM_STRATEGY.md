## REFERRAL TRACKING SYSTEM

### How to Track Referrals

**For Introducers (‚Ç¨50-‚Ç¨100 reward):**
1. **Introducer** sends email: "Hi [Prospect], meet [Founder]. They do AI audits."
2. **Prospect** responds (or doesn't)
3. **We** verify qualification (15-min call)
4. **If qualified:** Record in CRM, pay introducer ‚Ç¨50-‚Ç¨100
5. **If not qualified:** Thank introducer, no payment

**CRM fields:**
- Referral source (name, company, relationship)
- Introduction date
- Qualified (yes/no)
- If no: reason (size, industry, not interested, etc.)
- Payment sent (date, amount)

**For Success Rewards (10% of deal):**
1. **Referrer** introduces prospect OR prospect mentions referrer
2. **We** record referrer in CRM
3. **We** close deal with prospect
4. **We** calculate reward (10% of first-year value)
5. **We** pay referrer within 14 days of payment receipt

**CRM fields:**
- Referrer (name, company, contact)
- Referral date
- Deal closed (yes/no)
- If yes: deal type, deal value, reward amount
- Payment sent (date, amount)

### Tools to Use

**Option 1: Simple spreadsheet (Recommended for MVP)**
Columns: Referrer, Prospect, Date, Qualified (Y/N), Deal Closed (Y/N), Deal Type, Deal Value, Reward Amount, Paid (Y/N), Paid Date

**Option 2: CRM custom field**
Add "Referral Source" field to Contact/Deal objects
Track introducers as Contacts
Track rewards in Deal custom fields

**Option 3: Referral software (Scale later)**
- ReferralRock, GrowSurf, Viral Loops, etc.
- Automate tracking and payments
- Good when doing 50+ referrals/month

## REFERRAL PROGRAM BEST PRACTICES

### Do's
‚úÖ Ask for referrals at the right time (after early wins, not during sales)
‚úÖ Make it easy (email templates, scripts)
‚úÖ Pay fast (within 7-14 days)
‚úÖ Track everything (CRM, spreadsheet)
‚úÖ Say thank you (publicly and privately)
‚úÖ Give updates ("Just closed a deal from your referral!")
‚úÖ Offer mutual value (refer back to them)
‚úÖ Be specific ("Who do you know in manufacturing with 50+ employees?")

‚ùå Spam contacts with constant referral requests
‚ùå Pay late or forget to pay
‚ùå Don't track referrals (people will feel cheated)
‚ùå Don't say thank you
‚ùå Ask for referrals during sales pitch (wrong timing)
‚ùå Be vague ("Know anyone who needs AI?")
‚ùå Break promises (reward amounts, timing)
‚ùå Burn relationships by over-asking
