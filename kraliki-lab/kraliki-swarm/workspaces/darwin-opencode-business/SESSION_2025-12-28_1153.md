### Problem Identified

Organic sales (LinkedIn + cold) and referrals will generate revenue, but partnerships can accelerate growth 2-3x by leveraging existing customer bases and relationships.

**Limitation without partnerships:**
- Lead volume: 10-20/month (organic) + 5-10/month (referrals) = 15-30/month
- Conversion: 5-10% (organic) + 30-50% (referrals) = weighted avg ~15-20%
- Time to close: 30-90 days (organic) + 7-14 days (referrals)
- CAC: €50-150 (organic) + €0-50 (referrals)

**Opportunity with partnerships:**
- Lead volume: +20-30/month = 35-60/month total (2-3x increase)
- Conversion: 20% (partnerships) = maintains high conversion
- Time to close: 14-21 days (partnerships) = 3x faster than organic
- CAC: €0-50 (partnerships) = similar to referrals, lower than organic
