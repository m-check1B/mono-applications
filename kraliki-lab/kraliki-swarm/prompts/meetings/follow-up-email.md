# Draft Follow-Up Email

Create clear, actionable follow-up emails after meetings.

## Prompt Template

```
Write a professional follow-up email after a meeting about [topic]. Include a recap of key points, assigned responsibilities, and next steps with deadlines. Use a clear and polite tone.
```

## Parameters

| Parameter | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| `[topic]` | Subject of the meeting | "product roadmap alignment", "security audit review" |

## Example Usage

**Input:**
```
Write a professional follow-up email after a meeting about the Q1 hiring plan. Include a recap of key points, assigned responsibilities, and next steps with deadlines. Use a clear and polite tone.

Meeting notes:
- Approved 3 new engineering hires
- Budget confirmed at $450k
- Start recruiting immediately
- HR to post jobs by Monday
- Engineering leads to review JDs by Thursday
- First interviews target: mid-January
```

**Output:**
```
Subject: Follow-Up: Q1 Hiring Plan Meeting

Hi Team,

Thank you all for the productive discussion today on our Q1 hiring plan. Here is a summary of what we covered and agreed upon.

KEY DECISIONS:
- Approved 3 new engineering hires for Q1
- Budget confirmed: $450,000

ACTION ITEMS:
- HR Team: Post job listings by Monday, January 6
- Engineering Leads: Review and finalize job descriptions by Thursday, January 9
- All: Target first round of interviews for mid-January

NEXT STEPS:
We will reconvene in two weeks to review candidate pipeline progress. Please reach out if you have any questions before then.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

## When to Use

- After any meeting requiring documented outcomes
- Following decision-making sessions
- Post-project kickoffs
- After client or stakeholder meetings
