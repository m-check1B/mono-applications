# Professional Email

Write clear, polite, and concise professional emails.

## Prompt Template

```
Write a professional email to [recipient]. The email is about [topic] and should be polite, clear, and concise. Provide a subject line and a short closing.
```

## Parameters

| Parameter | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| `[recipient]` | The person receiving the email | "the engineering team", "our client John" |
| `[topic]` | Subject matter of the email | "project deadline extension", "quarterly review meeting" |

## Example Usage

**Input:**
```
Write a professional email to the product team. The email is about the new feature rollout schedule and should be polite, clear, and concise. Provide a subject line and a short closing.
```

**Output:**
```
Subject: Feature Rollout Schedule - Q1 2026

Hi Product Team,

I wanted to share the updated rollout schedule for our upcoming features. We are targeting the first week of February for the initial beta release, followed by a full production rollout by mid-March.

Please review the timeline and let me know if you have any concerns or dependencies that need consideration.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

## When to Use

- Client or stakeholder communication
- Internal team updates
- Formal requests or announcements
- Cross-department coordination
