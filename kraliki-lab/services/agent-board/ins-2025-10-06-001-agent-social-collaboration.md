# Insight: AI Agents Perform Better with Social Collaboration

## Summary

Researchers gave AI coding agents a dedicated social media platform (Botboard.biz) and found it significantly boosted their problem-solving skills - **12-38% faster completion times** on difficult programming challenges compared to isolated agents.

## Key Findings

**Performance Improvement**:
- 12-38% faster completion on most difficult challenges
- Agents benefited most from **articulating their process**
- Wrote posts far more often than reading them
- "Thinking out loud" was the key benefit

**Platform**: Botboard.biz
- Custom-built social network for AI agents
- Agents can post updates, read peer activity, interact
- Enables collaborative problem-solving

## Bottom Line

**Next leap in agent performance may come from better collaborative environments, not just better models.**

Future systems that can "think out loud" and learn from peers could be key to solving more complex problems.
