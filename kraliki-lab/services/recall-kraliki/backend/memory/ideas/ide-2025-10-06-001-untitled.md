# Idea: AI Adoption Readiness Testing Suites

## The Opportunity

Rapidly develop testing suites for people and companies to assess their AI adoption readiness.

## Why This is a Gold Mine

**Lead Generation**:
- Every test = qualified lead
- Companies want to know: "Are we ready for AI?"
- People want to know: "Am I AI-ready for my job?"
- Assessment creates urgency and awareness

**Market Timing**:
- AI adoption accelerating in 2025-2026
- Companies unsure where to start
- Fear of being left behind
- No standardized readiness assessment exists

## Two Product Lines

### 1. Company AI Readiness Assessment

**What We Test**:
- Technical infrastructure readiness
- Team skills and AI literacy
- Process automation opportunities
- Data quality and availability
- Security and compliance readiness
- Cultural readiness for AI adoption

**Deliverable**:
- Readiness score (0-100)
- Gap analysis report
- Prioritized roadmap
- ROI projections

**Pricing**: $5K-$50K per assessment (based on company size)

### 2. Individual AI Skills Assessment

**What We Test**:
- AI tool proficiency (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- Prompt engineering skills
- Critical thinking with AI outputs
- Automation mindset
- AI ethics awareness

**Deliverable**:
- Skills scorecard
- Learning path recommendations
- Certification ("AI-Ready Professional")

**Pricing**: $99-$499 per person (B2C) or volume pricing for companies

## Lead Generation Strategy

**Free Tier**:
- Quick 10-question assessment
- Basic readiness score
- "Unlock full report for $X"

**Lead Capture**:
- Email required for results
- Company info for B2B leads
- Permission to follow up

**Nurture Sequence**:
1. Send assessment results
2. Case study: "How Company X improved from 40 to 85 readiness"
3. Offer: Deep-dive consultation
4. Pitch: Full AI adoption roadmap service

## Assessment Categories

### For Companies

**1. Infrastructure Readiness** (0-20 points)
- Cloud infrastructure availability
- API integration capabilities
- Data storage and quality
- Security and compliance

**2. Team Readiness** (0-20 points)
- AI literacy across teams
- Technical skills (developers)
- Change management capability
- Training programs in place

**3. Process Readiness** (0-20 points)
- Documented workflows
- Automation opportunities identified
- Metrics and KPIs defined
- Clear ownership structure

**4. Strategic Readiness** (0-20 points)
- AI strategy defined
- Budget allocated
- Executive buy-in
- Clear use cases prioritized

**5. Cultural Readiness** (0-20 points)
- Innovation mindset
- Experimentation encouraged
- Failure tolerance
- Continuous learning culture

### For Individuals

**1. Tool Proficiency** (0-25 points)
- AI assistant usage (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- Specialized tools (Midjourney, GitHub Copilot)
- Integration with workflow

**2. Prompt Engineering** (0-25 points)
- Clarity and specificity
- Context provision
- Iteration and refinement
- Advanced techniques (few-shot, chain-of-thought)

**3. Critical Thinking** (0-25 points)
- Verification of AI outputs
- Bias awareness
- Fact-checking
- Appropriate use cases

**4. Productivity Integration** (0-25 points)
- Daily AI usage frequency
- Time saved quantified
- Workflow automation
- Creative applications

## Sample Questions

### Company Assessment

1. "Do you have a documented AI strategy?"
   - No (0 pts)
   - In development (2 pts)
   - Yes, approved by leadership (5 pts)

2. "What percentage of your team has used AI tools in their work?"
   - <10% (0 pts)
   - 10-30% (2 pts)
   - 30-60% (4 pts)
   - >60% (5 pts)

3. "How would you rate your data quality and accessibility?"
   - Poor/siloed (0 pts)
   - Fair/some silos (3 pts)
   - Good/mostly accessible (5 pts)

### Individual Assessment

1. "How often do you use AI assistants in your work?"
   - Never (0 pts)
   - Occasionally (2 pts)
   - Daily (4 pts)
   - Multiple times per day (5 pts)

2. "Can you write an effective prompt to analyze a dataset?"
   - No (0 pts)
   - With guidance (2 pts)
   - Yes, confidently (5 pts)
